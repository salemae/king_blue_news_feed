KingBlue App Help

Welcome to the KingBlue App!

The KingBlue App is your personal RSS Feed Notifier that helps you stay updated with your favorite content. Here are the steps to get started:

  1. Add RSS Feeds: To receive updates from your favorite websites, add RSS feed sources under "Manage Feeds." You can find RSS feeds on websites like The Hacker News.
  2. Setup SMTP Configuration: Configure your SMTP settings under "SMTP" for email notifications. If you don't have an SMTP server, you can use services like SendGrid and Mailgun.
  3. Set the Email Delivery Schedule: Schedule when you want to receive email notifications under "Email Delivery."

Enjoy using the KingBlue App and stay informed!
